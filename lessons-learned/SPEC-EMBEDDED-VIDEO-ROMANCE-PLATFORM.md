# FEATURE SPEC — Embedded Video Romance Platform

> Generated by The Whisperer — April 25, 2026

## What it does
A publishing platform where creators write adult romance stories with short video clips embedded inline at specific moments in the prose, giving female readers emotional and visual connection to the story simultaneously.

## How it gets triggered
A reader finds a story, starts reading. At certain sentences a clickable link appears inline. They click it or don't — their choice.

## What happens when it works
Reader is moving through the prose. A sentence like "he slowly raised her skirt" has a link. They click it. A popup plays a short clip of exactly that moment. They close it and keep reading. The story never stops. The video is an enhancement not an interruption.

## What happens when it breaks
Reader clicks a link and nothing loads. Reader tries to access paid content without buying. Creator uploads a clip that doesn't match the moment in the prose. Payment fails at checkout.

## States it can be in
- Free preview — up to 5 clips, no nudity, anyone can read
- Locked — everything past the free threshold requires purchase
- Purchased — full story, all clips, fully unlocked

## What it touches
Creator publishing tools. Video upload and clip management. Payment and access control. Story reader interface.

## Edge cases
- Reader on mobile — popup video behavior is different
- Creator uploads more than 5 clips — what happens to the extras?
- Reader buys the book then requests a refund
- Creator tries to embed nudity in a free clip

## Definition of done
- A reader can open a story and read it straight through without clicking anything
- A reader can click an embedded link and a short video plays in a popup without leaving the page
- After the 5th free clip, the reader sees a purchase prompt
- After purchase, all clips unlock
- A creator can write a story, embed up to 5 free clips and unlimited paid clips, and publish it
